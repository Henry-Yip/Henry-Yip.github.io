    I am disgusted by myself…

    I do have the motivation to do well. I am involved in Research Groups, I applied for many interns (which, btw, is about to reject me). But I am still struggling in stress problems, I have inefficient revision methods, I tend not to do well.

    I want to be a physicist but I can’t code other than elementary Python. I struggle in pure math, I can’t find the intuition in applied math (or maybe something like dynamics)

    I want to play violin well but I lacked the motivation and when I want to join the orchestra I am rejected (well obviously)

    I want to play piano but there isn’t a piano nearby and therefore I can’t play (even if I do I will lack motivation real fast)

    I want to fix this website but the content is quite bad I don’t even know where to start (Hey Thanks if you think otherwise!)

    How can I even change this?

    Maybe a better timetable? Maybe more relaxation? But if I relax more how do I possibly, like possibly, get the time to do research? And read Hyperloop stuff?

    Beyond the seemingly decent grades I get what do I want?

    It’s disgusting. What I am doing, how I am graudally lacking motivation.

    How do I meet new friends or even keep remaining friends if I don’t socialize?

    How do I balance everything?

    How do I maintain my fitness in running/ at least keep myself healthy?

    Time is ticking… I better figure these out before Year 3!

    Time is ticking… How much time do I have?
